Automated Attendance

An innovative solution designed specifically for rural educational institutions to streamline attendance tracking using modern technology.

Key Features

Our system is designed with rural schools in mind, addressing unique challenges with innovative solutions.

Face Recognition

Face scanning technology for accurate and secure student identification.

Real-time Analytics

Monitor attendance patterns and generate insightful reports for school administration.

Mobile Accessibility

Works on low-end smartphones with limited internet connectivity common in rural areas.

Low Power Consumption

Optimized to work efficiently even with frequent power outages.

Multi-language Support

Available in multiple regional languages to cater to diverse rural communities.

Offline Functionality

Works without internet connection and syncs data when connectivity is available.
